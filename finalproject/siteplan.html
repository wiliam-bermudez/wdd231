SITE PLAN

DelSalvafood - for parties

The site provides you with a brief explanation of the "DelSalvafood - for Parties" website. Explaining its main services, contact information and the most important, the great experiences our customers have had with us.

Site Purpose

The website provides with more informaation about DelSalvafood - for parties, a business that aims to bring trully salvadorean food experiences to the parties around Mexico. It shows some of the top dishes of El Salvador and its culture in order to feel it. It provides information about the business, schedules and offers.

Scenarios

  • What is the best salvadorean food ever to have in a party?
  • Where can I get good salvadorean food like "pupusas" for my birthday party?

Color Schema

Selected colors and their usage across the site:

  • Primary color: #086788ff — used for headings and navigation elements.
  • Secondary color: #f0c808 — used for buttons, highlights, and call-to-action sections.
  • Background color: #rgb(236, 225, 197) — main page background.
  • Text color: #1b1b1b — used for paragraphs and body text.
