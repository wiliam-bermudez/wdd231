Membership Application

Membership Levels

NP Membership

NP Membership

Joining our Chamber of Commerce as a Non-Profit Member provides your organization with valuable networking opportunities, increased visibility, and access to resources designed to support your mission. As a member, you'll be able to collaborate with other local non-profits, businesses, and community leaders to amplify your impact, advocate for causes important to your organization, and access exclusive training, events, and discounts. Our Chamber is committed to strengthening the local economy and community, and by becoming a member, your non-profit will be part of a dynamic network dedicated to collective growth and success.

Bronze Membership

Bronze Membership

As a Bronze Member of our Chamber of Commerce, your business will gain essential support and visibility within the local business community. This membership provides access to networking opportunities, promotional listings, and invitations to Chamber events. Bronze members also receive access to valuable resources, such as basic business training and educational materials, helping you strengthen your business foundation. This level is perfect for small businesses or startups looking to build connections and grow within the local market.

Silver Membership

Silver Membership

As a Silver Member of our Chamber of Commerce, your business will gain increased visibility, access to exclusive networking events, and the opportunity to connect with local leaders and potential partners. You'll receive promotional support, access to business resources like workshops and training, and priority invitations to key Chamber events. This level is perfect for businesses looking to expand their influence, engage with the local community, and enhance their growth potential.

Gold Membership

Gold Membership

The Gold Membership offers the highest level of support and visibility. As a Gold Member, your business will enjoy all the benefits of the Silver Membership, plus exclusive opportunities for high-profile promotion, premier networking access, and priority placement in Chamber events. You’ll also have access to specialized resources and one-on-one consultation with Chamber leaders to help accelerate your business strategy. This membership is ideal for businesses looking for maximum exposure, influence, and growth opportunities in the community.
